This week, I was honored to be the keynote for Gusto’s President’s Club in Cancun! A first for me and such a cool idea to give to their top performers.

A few things I loved:

- In addition to the larger Penny’s Club winners, this was their first time celebrating Penny’s Circle winners, who are 4x Club winners or top of their categories for last fiscal. 

- They created a market full of local goods and surprised each attendee with $150 to spend. 

- We facilitated a discussion with a handful of the winners to share super specific tactics on what made them successful this year. “Maintaining the BDR mindset” was a favorite share. 

- We agreed someone would corral those notes and host a webinar for all sellers to learn from what was shared. 

- One of their core values is Debate and Commit. Let’s debate change, perspective, etc and then commit to the plan moving forward. Such an awesome way to be heard and then problem solve an action plan. 

- The swag, the sweet gifts delivered to the room, and all the thoughtful words shared about their peers, their bosses, and the unseen work the leaders do each day. 

The authentcity and positive culture can really be felt, and not just in this room of gleeful top performers. I am a Gustomer, as is the lingo, and have had such a positive experience with the platform and service - was a full circle moment to be here. 

Congrats to all the winners - thanks for making me feel so welcome, and to our planning partners for being impossibly lovely to work with these last few months!
