How do you follow up without being pushy?

Shift from asking to giving. 

Here’s how.

Write down ten questions your prospects usually ask.
Or the ones they should be asking but rarely do.

Answer them.

If you’re comfortable on video, record a short response.
Two minutes.
No pitch.
Just clarity.

Then use those answers as your follow-ups.

One question.
One answer.
One email per month.

This post is an example.

Why this works:

You’re teaching instead of pressuring.
You position yourself as someone who knows the space.
Your personality comes through, not just your product.

You’re no longer “following up.”
You’re making people smarter about topics they care about.

Don’t remind them you exist.
Give them a reason to care.

Teach first.
Trust follows.
