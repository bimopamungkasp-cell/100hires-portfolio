Sorry, Claude Code purists- Claude Design is actually great too. 

This is a PSA for non-technical people who don’t have the budget to hire a GTM Engineer: just use Claude Design. Stop banging your head at Claude Code to generate consistently good marketing and sales assets when you can get 90% of the way there with a significantly easier tool. 

I think GTM Engineers tend to over-complicate things. We prefer to think of how to build the very best system, but sometimes not the most practical one. Adoption is 80% of any workflow. If people can’t use what you’re building, then it’s not worth building. 

I love Claude Code, but for someone who hasn’t touched AI tools before, it can be a little intimidating. You’re using MCPs, APIs, writing things to Github, you get the idea. 

Claude Design is super simple, requires very little technical acumen, and you can start creating beautiful slide decks, social assets, and more in just minutes.
