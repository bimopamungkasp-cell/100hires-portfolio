Here are 3 simple Claude Skills you should build for your sales team:

1. Post-Call CRM Deal Updates

Almost anything you could possibly need to update a deal in your CRM can be derived from a call transcript and emails. The main things we care about- current stage, next steps, and when to follow up. All you need is three MCPs- one for your call transcript software (we use Fathom.ai), one for your email, and one for your CRM. Then, just test in a sandbox to ensure the deals are getting updated perfectly every time with standardized data so you don’t get smacked by your Head of RevOps. 

2. Deck Builder

Sales teams should never be building sales assets anymore. This skill should contain: the MCP of your preferred deck software (we use Google Slides), a link to the best-looking slide deck template you have, and instructions on how a sales deck at your company should be structured. Then, use an enrichment tool like Deepline or Parallel Web Systems to perform third party research on the company. After that, use your call transcript MCPs to inject any first party info you’ve gathered from the prospect. After a few tweaks, all your sales team has to do is make minor edits. 

3. Daily Sales Brief

If you built the first skill in this list, then this one is a natural progression. Your CRM (if you have a good GTM Engineer on your team) should have literally everything your team needs to know on a deal; when to follow up, what next steps from calls were, and, in short, what they should prioritize in their day. A well-built Claude skill attached to a read-only CRM MCP can give you everything an AE should work on that day in 30 seconds. 

We have around a dozen skills internally, so if you want me to keep making posts like this (or show how we’ve built them for ourselves internally), let me know!
