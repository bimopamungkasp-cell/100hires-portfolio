Claude Code runs our entire cold outbound, signal to live campaign. 

One terminal, any API, fully personalized. The full build:

Outbound tools lock you into their data and their steps. Claude Code connects to any tool with an API, so the whole thing lives in one place.

It starts with a signal: a new VP of Sales, fresh funding, a competitor getting compared on G2. That is the reason to reach out now.

The flow, signal to sent:

→ The signal fires. A script scores the account and ranks it, so nobody guesses who to work.

→ Apollo finds the buying committee. An enrichment waterfall returns the verified email, cheapest source first.

→ Claude writes the email against the signal, in your own framework. Outreach that names the signal pulls 3-4x more replies than generic.

→ The campaign loads into Instantly and goes live, all through the API.

→ Replies feed back in. The copy that won gets reused on the next run.

All of it reads from one folder: your ICP, your scoring rules, your best-performing copy. Claude opens those files before every task: better files, better output.

Once a workflow lands, you save it and run it again. Hiring signals to a live sequence. G2 shoppers to a steal campaign. Anonymous site visitors to real outbound.

This is the same engine we run at ColdIQ.
