Okay, so now the kitchen remodeling industry is in the cold outreach game? 😂 

When you're building your campaigns... 

Ask yourself, "What is the likelihood that this person is ready to buy my service now?" 

Then, adjust your CTA accordingly. 

For example, in this silly SMS below, I’d probably change:

“Want a quote?”

to:

“Want to see a few design ideas your neighbors have been going with?”

Lower friction. More curiosity. Less “are you ready to buy today?”

Happy Friday.

P.S. Don't cold SMS b2c buyers unless you want a flood of angry replies.
