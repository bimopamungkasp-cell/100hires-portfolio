I've stopped trying to "figure out" LinkedIn’s algorithm. 

A post I almost trashed -- the one I wrote in 6 min on a Friday afternoon -- was the one LI decided to show to a *relatively* wide audience. 

The takeaway? 

Post only to share notes on what you're learning. 
Let go of LI content as a lead source (for a while).
But stay consistent.

Your writing will improve. 
Ideas will flow more easily. 

And you’ll save yourself a lot of stress wondering why certain posts “should have” done better.
