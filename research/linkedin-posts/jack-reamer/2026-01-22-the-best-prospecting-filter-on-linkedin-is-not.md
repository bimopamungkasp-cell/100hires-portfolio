The best prospecting filter on LinkedIn is not on Sales Navigator.

It’s free for everyone on LinkedIn’s basic search. And you’re probably not taking full advantage of it.

I’m talking about the “Followers of” filter. 

You're thinking: “Ok, so I can use this to find people based on who they follow on LinkedIn. 

What’s the big deal?”

It lets you build a list of buyers who already believe in what you’re selling.

For example, let’s say you offer cold calling coaching. 

Which list would book more meetings?

List #1) Sales VPs at companies with 250-500 employees.
List #2) Sales VPs at companies with 250-500 employees who follow at least 3 cold calling experts on LinkedIn. 

The winner is obvious. It gives you a strong indication they not only do cold calling, but are actively trying to improve their scripts and results. 

Pro tip: For best list quality, enrich the profiles you find (you know which tools do this) to verify things like company size, industry, location, seniority, etc. 

P.S. LinkedIn filters come and go. (Remember when you could see members in every group on SalesNav?) So don’t sleep on this one.
